ABOUT US

Company
"Agrarian Production House of Ukraine"
is a leading supplier of grains, pulses and oilseeds throughout Ukraine and an exporter of these products abroad.

We have years of experience in wholesale procurement, storage, and processing of grain crops, which allows us to ensure high quality products and competitive prices.

In addition, we import compound feed from leading European manufacturers and produce high-quality animal feed blends. Our products meet all quality and safety standards, making them popular among domestic farmers and agriculture. We have a wide network of suppliers and partners, which allows us to respond quickly to market changes and meet the needs of our customers at any time.

Our company values mutually beneficial business relationships with our partners, which is why we are always ready to collaborate with new clients and suppliers. We operate with high standards of quality and ethics, which makes our company a reliable and trustworthy partner.
